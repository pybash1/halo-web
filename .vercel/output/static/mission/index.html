Our Mission
We believe safety shouldn't be a luxury or something you have to worry about when you're just trying to live your life.
Our mission is simple: give people the tools they need to feel secure and get help fast when it matters most. Whether someone is walking home alone, in an unsafe relationship, or facing any threat to their wellbeing, they deserve more than just hoping for the best.
We're building Halo to bridge that gap between feeling vulnerable and feeling protected. Real safety features that work when you need them, designed by people who actually understand what it's like to feel unsafe.
Everyone should be able to move through the world with confidence, knowing that help is always within reach. That's not just our goal - it's our responsibility.
