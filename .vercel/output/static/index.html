Our Mission

We believe safety should not be a luxury or something you have to worry about when you are just trying to live your life.

Our mission is simple: give people the tools they need to feel secure and get help fast when it matters most. Whether someone is walking home alone, in an unsafe relationship, or facing any threat to their wellbeing, they deserve more than just hoping for the best.

We are building Halo to bridge that gap between feeling vulnerable and feeling protected. Real safety features that work when you need them, designed by people who understand what it is like to feel unsafe.

Everyone should be able to move through the world with confidence, knowing that help is always within reach.

About Us

We are Ananjan and Ritankar, two friends who decided we could not just stand by while people we care about face real safety threats every day.

After hearing too many stories from friends and family about feeling unsafe walking alone or being in dangerous situations without help, we realized the tools out there were missing what people need in critical moments.

We are building Halo because everyone deserves to feel safe and have real help when they need it.

HALO

Founded by Ananjan Mitra and Ritankar Bhunia, Halo is being built to make personal safety faster, simpler, and genuinely reliable when people need support the most.
